# VOM Agency

**VOM** is a digital agency with the sole purpose of connecting local high quality stores with a wider online audience, providing a forum to increase visibility, sales, and consumer engagement.

VOM Agency services include:

- Custom website, optimally coded
- Online domain and hosting service
- Direct contact link to the business owner/seller for customers to reach out
- An AI Receptionist to automate customer service
- Long-term consistent management of your domain
